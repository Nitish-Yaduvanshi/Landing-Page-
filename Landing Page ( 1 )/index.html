RORONOA ZORO

THE KING OF HELL

Roronoa Zoro, the "Pirate Hunter," is the swordsman of the Straw Hat Pirates in *One Piece*. As a master of the Three-Sword Style (Santoryu), he wields unmatched strength and dreams of becoming the world's greatest swordsman. Known for his green hair, iconic swords, and comical lack of direction, Zoro is fiercely loyal, stoic, and a powerhouse in battle, making him a fan-favorite character in anime.
